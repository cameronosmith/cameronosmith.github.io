This site is mostly just a journal - not a diary about what's going on in my
life, but a place to try to define what's giving me anxiety about life and the
human condition. It's mostly for myself to try to define and externalize these
anxieties rather than have them swirling in my conscious and subconscious, but
it may help you to know that someone else is experiencing the same anxieties and
feel less like a freakazoid.
